Explainer: What Is a Consent Agenda (and Why It Matters)?

A consent agenda groups routine items into a single vote to save time—think approval of minutes, standard contracts, or recurring payments.

Why you should care: Occasionally non-routine items end up bundled there. If a member pulls an item from consent, it gets discussed and voted on separately.

  • Look for “consent” or “consent agenda” near the top of the agenda.
  • If you see something substantial, ask a member to request it be pulled for discussion.
