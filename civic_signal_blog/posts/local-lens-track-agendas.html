Local Lens: How to Track Your City Council Agenda in 60 Seconds

  1. Google: “YOUR CITY council agenda” or “clerk meeting packet.”
  2. Bookmark the page that posts PDFs (often the Clerk or Meetings page).
  3. Skim the consent agenda and new business; open attachments for key items.
  4. Copy the URL into your notes with the meeting date and a one-line summary.
One-minute checklist
  • When/where?
  • Votes planned?
  • Impact on taxes, services, zoning?

You can adapt this for county commissions, school boards, and planning commissions, too.
