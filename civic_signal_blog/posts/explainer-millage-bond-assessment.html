Explainer: Millages, Bond Proposals, and Special Assessments—Plain English

Millage: A property tax rate (often expressed in mills) that funds operations like roads, libraries, or safety services.

Bond Proposal: Allows a government to borrow money for big projects (e.g., buildings), repaid over time—often with dedicated property taxes.

Special Assessment: A charge on properties that benefit from a specific improvement (like a sidewalk or street lighting district).

Details vary by state and local law—always refer to your city, township, or county’s official explanations.
